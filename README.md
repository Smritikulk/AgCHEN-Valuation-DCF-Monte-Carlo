# AgCHEN-Valuation-DCF-Monte-Carlo
DCF and Monte Carlo valuation model for AgCHEN Inc. to estimate intrinsic value, analyze uncertainty in revenue growth, environmental liabilities, and tax rates, and evaluate acquisition feasibility using risk-adjusted financial modeling.

Results:
- Estimated enterprise value of approximately $13.03B using perpetuity growth and $11.82B using exit multiple valuation.
- Implied intrinsic share price range of approximately $78.62–$88.30 compared with a current market price of $57.00.
- Monte Carlo simulation showed most share price outcomes clustered between $70–$90.
- Environmental liabilities were identified as the primary valuation risk factor.
- Recommended conservative acquisition threshold: approximately $75/share.
